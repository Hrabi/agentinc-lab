# Contoso Electronics — Q4 2025 Quarterly Report

## Executive Summary

Q4 2025 was a strong quarter for Contoso Electronics, with total revenue of **€47.3 million**, representing a **12% year-over-year increase**. The Smart Home division continued its growth trajectory, driven by the SmartHub 400 launch in October. Industrial IoT saw steady adoption in the manufacturing sector. Consumer Audio exceeded expectations during the holiday season.

---

## Revenue Breakdown

| Division | Q4 2025 (€M) | Q4 2024 (€M) | YoY Change |
|----------|--------------|--------------|------------|
| Smart Home | 22.1 | 18.3 | +20.8% |
| Industrial IoT | 14.7 | 13.9 | +5.8% |
| Consumer Audio | 10.5 | 9.9 | +6.1% |
| **Total** | **47.3** | **42.1** | **+12.4%** |

### Smart Home (46.7% of revenue)

The SmartHub 400, launched on October 1, sold **38,000 units** in its first quarter — exceeding the forecast of 30,000 by 27%. Key growth drivers:

- **Matter protocol support** attracted customers from competitor ecosystems
- Bundle promotions (SmartHub + 3 sensors for €299) drove a **42% attach rate** for accessories
- Strong reviews (4.7★ average across major retailers) generated organic demand

The TempSense Pro sensor saw a **15% volume increase** due to energy-crisis-driven demand for home monitoring.

### Industrial IoT (31.1% of revenue)

Industrial IoT revenue grew modestly at 5.8%. Notable developments:

- **VibraSense X1** secured a 3-year contract with **Bosch Manufacturing** for predictive maintenance across 12 European plants (€2.1M annual contract value)
- FlowMeter M3 was certified for MID (Measuring Instruments Directive), opening the utilities market
- Pipeline: 14 enterprise trials in progress, expected to close in Q1-Q2 2026

### Consumer Audio (22.2% of revenue)

Holiday season drove strong consumer audio sales:

- SoundBuds NC3: **62,000 units** sold (+35% vs Q3), best-selling product by volume
- SoundPillar S5: **4,200 units** sold, a premium product with high margin (68% gross margin)
- Total consumer audio gross margin: **54%** (up from 51% in Q3)

## Outlook — Q1 2026

### Priorities

1. **SmartLock BT2 launch** (March 2026) — pre-orders already at 8,500 units
2. **Industrial IoT expansion** — close 8+ enterprise trials, target €5M in new annual contracts
3. **ContosoGuard extended warranty** — aim for 25% attach rate on all products
4. **Cost optimization** — renegotiate component contracts to improve gross margin by 1-2pp

### Risks

- **Semiconductor supply:** Lead times for Bluetooth 5.4 chipsets remain at 16 weeks
- **Competition:** Apple and Google are expanding smart home offerings with Matter
- **Currency:** EUR/USD volatility may impact North American margins

### Revenue Guidance

**Q1 2026:** €44-46M (typically softer quarter post-holiday)  
**Full Year 2026:** €195-210M (15-22% growth)
