# Contoso Electronics — Employee & Policy Handbook

**Version:** 2026.1  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Department:** Human Resources

---

## 1. Company Overview

Contoso Electronics is a global provider of smart home devices, industrial IoT sensors, and consumer electronics. Founded in 2008 in Amsterdam, the company employs approximately 4,200 people across 12 offices in Europe, North America, and Asia-Pacific.

Our mission is to make intelligent technology accessible, sustainable, and secure for every household and business.

---

## 2. Working Hours & Flexibility

### Standard Hours
The standard work week is **40 hours**, Monday through Friday.  
Core hours are **10:00–15:00 CET** — all employees must be available (or have manager approval for absence) during core hours.

### Flexible Working
- Employees may start between 07:00 and 10:00 and end between 15:00 and 19:00.
- **Remote work:** Up to 3 days per week, subject to team agreement.
- **Compressed weeks:** 4 × 10-hour days available with manager approval.

### Overtime
Overtime above 40 hours requires prior written approval from the department head. Overtime is compensated at **1.5× the hourly rate** for weekdays and **2× the hourly rate** for weekends and public holidays.

## 3. Leave Policy

### Annual Leave
- **Standard:** 25 working days per year (pro-rata for part-time).
- **Senior staff (5+ years):** 28 working days.
- **Unused leave:** Up to 5 days may carry over to the next year. Remaining days expire on March 31.

### Sick Leave
- Employees receive **full pay for the first 10 sick days** per calendar year.
- Days 11–30 are paid at **70% of base salary**.
- A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding **3 consecutive days**.

### Parental Leave
- **Primary caregiver:** 16 weeks at full pay + up to 10 additional weeks at 70%.
- **Partner leave:** 6 weeks at full pay.
- Parental leave must be requested at least 8 weeks before the expected date.

### Other Leave Types
| Type | Duration | Paid? |
|------|----------|-------|
| Bereavement (immediate family) | 5 days | Yes |
| Bereavement (extended family) | 2 days | Yes |
| Marriage/civil partnership | 3 days | Yes |
| Moving house | 1 day | Yes |
| Jury duty | As required | Yes |
| Volunteering | 2 days/year | Yes |

## 4. Return & Warranty Policy (Products)

### Customer Returns
- Products may be returned within **30 calendar days** of delivery with proof of purchase.
- Items must be in **original packaging** and in **unused or like-new condition**.
- Refunds are processed within **5–7 business days** to the original payment method.
- **Opened software/firmware licenses** are non-refundable.
- Shipping costs for returns are covered by Contoso only if the item is defective.

### Warranty
- All consumer electronics carry a **2-year manufacturer warranty** from date of purchase.
- Industrial IoT sensors carry a **3-year warranty**.
- Warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship but excludes damage from misuse, unauthorized modifications, or natural disasters.
- Warranty claims require the original invoice and product serial number.

### Extended Warranty (ContosoGuard)
- Customers may purchase ContosoGuard for an additional 1–3 years of coverage.
- ContosoGuard must be purchased within 30 days of the original product purchase.
- ContosoGuard covers accidental damage (drops, spills) with a €50 deductible per claim.

## 5. Expense Policy

### Travel
- **Domestic flights:** Economy class. Business class requires VP approval.
- **International flights:** Economy for flights under 6 hours; business class for flights 6+ hours.
- **Hotels:** Up to €180/night in standard cities, up to €250/night in high-cost cities (London, Zurich, New York, Tokyo, Singapore).
- **Meals:** Up to €60/day. Alcohol is reimbursable up to €15/day during business dinners only.
- **Rental cars:** Compact or midsize. SUVs require justification.

### Equipment
- Employees may request a laptop refresh every 3 years.
- Software licenses must be requested through IT ServiceDesk (JIRA ticket category: IT-SOFTWARE).
- Monitor and peripheral requests up to €500 are auto-approved by the direct manager.

### Reimbursement Process
1. Submit receipts via **Contoso Expense Portal** within 30 days of the expense.
2. Receipts over €25 require a photo/scan of the original receipt.
3. Manager approval is required for individual expenses over €200.
4. Finance processes approved expenses in the next payroll cycle.

## 6. Information Security

### Data Classification
| Level | Description | Examples |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Public** | No restriction | Marketing materials, press releases |
| **Internal** | Company-only | Org charts, internal memos, meeting notes |
| **Confidential** | Need-to-know | Customer PII, financial reports, roadmaps |
| **Restricted** | Named access | Trade secrets, M&A data, encryption keys |

### Password Policy
- Minimum **14 characters**, at least one uppercase, one lowercase, one digit, one symbol.
- Passwords expire every **90 days**.
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is **mandatory** for all employees.
- Password managers are recommended; Contoso provides enterprise Bitwarden licenses.

### Acceptable Use
- Company devices may be used for **limited personal use** during breaks.
- Installing unauthorized software is prohibited.
- All data on company devices is subject to monitoring and audit.
- USB storage devices are **blocked by policy**. Use OneDrive or SharePoint for file transfer.

## 7. Performance Reviews

- Reviews occur **twice per year**: mid-year (June) and end-of-year (December).
- The review process uses a **360-degree feedback model** (self, peers, manager, reports).
- Rating scale: **1 (Below Expectations)** → **5 (Exceptional)**.
- Performance improvement plans (PIPs) are issued for ratings of 1 or 2 in two consecutive cycles.
- Promotions require a rating of 4+ in the last two consecutive review cycles.

## 8. Code of Conduct

1. Treat all colleagues, customers, and partners with **respect and dignity**.
2. **No discrimination** based on age, gender, ethnicity, religion, disability, sexual orientation, or any protected characteristic.
3. Report conflicts of interest to your manager and HR within **5 business days** of becoming aware.
4. **Gifts:** Do not accept gifts valued over €50 from vendors or partners. Report all gifts to Compliance.
5. **Confidentiality:** Do not share proprietary information outside the company without written authorization.
6. **Whistleblowing:** Report unethical behavior through the anonymous Ethics Hotline or directly to the Chief Compliance Officer.
